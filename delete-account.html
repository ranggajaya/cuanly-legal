Delete Account - Cuanly

Users may permanently delete their account directly from within the Cuanly application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Cuanly app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

What Data Is Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Email account association
  • Wallets
  • Transactions
  • Categories
  • Budgets
  • Recurring transactions

Retention Policy

Data is permanently removed after account deletion and cannot be recovered.
